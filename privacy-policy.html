What Information We Collect?

To provide you with the best shopping experience, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Full Name, Phone Number, Email Address
  • Delivery Information: Address, Thana, District, Division
  • Order Information: Products Purchased, Quantity, Total Amount
  • Technical Data: IP Address, Browser Type, Device Information

How We Use Your Information?

  • To process and deliver your orders successfully
  • To provide order updates and customer support
  • To improve our website experience and services

  • To prevent fraud and ensure platform security
Important: We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party. All your data is securely stored within HUTBAZ and only accessible by authorized Admin.

Cookies Policy

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us remember your cart, login information, and preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

Data Security

We use Firebase's enterprise-grade security to protect your data. All information is encrypted and stored securely. Only HUTBAZ Admin has access to customer data for order processing and support purposes.
